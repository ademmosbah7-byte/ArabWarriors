# Arab Warriors

Welcome to the Arab Warriors faction! This mod adds 7 legendary warriors inspired by Middle Eastern culture and mythology to TABS!

### If you have trouble with the mod, or if you just want to chat, you can join the [TABS Mod Center](https://discord.gg/zrs44qyp7S).

### This mod is [**open source**](https://github.com/your-username/ArabWarriors).

## How To Use

The Arab Warriors faction can be found in the factions bar. Have fun conquering with these legendary units!

## Arab Warriors Units

| Unit | Cost | HP | Description |
|-|-|-|-|
| **Gun Man** | 150 | 100 | Armed with a powerful Desert Eagle pistol, this marksman delivers devastating shots from range! |
| **Plastek Man** | 200 | 100 | A rubber warrior who can stretch his arms to attack enemies from medium range! |
| **Best** | 400 | 300 | This beast leaps high into the air and explodes on landing, crushing groups of enemies! |
| **Black Tiger** | 300 | 200 | The fastest unit in the game! This agile tiger dodges 50% of attacks and strikes with deadly bites. |
| **Knight Arabe** | 600 | 500 | A noble knight with sword and shield. When his health drops below 50%, he enters Rage Mode for increased damage and speed! |
| **King Arabe** | 1500 | 1250 | The legendary Arab King wields a flaming sword that shoots fire projectiles, burning enemies for massive damage over time! |
| ***EXODIA*** | 9999 | 5000 | The Forbidden One! This colossal 3.5x giant unleashes devastating shockwaves that obliterate everything in a 25-meter radius! |

## Secret Units

| Unit | Location | Description |
|-|-|-|
| **Exodia** | *Complete all 20 campaign missions* | Unlock this legendary boss unit to use in battle! The ultimate weapon with 5000 HP and earth-shattering power. |

## Campaign

The mod includes an epic 20-mission campaign:

- **Missions 1-5**: Training missions to learn each unit
- **Missions 6-10**: Increasing difficulty battles
- **Missions 11-15**: Elite challenges
- **Missions 16-19**: Ultimate tests of strategy
- **Mission 20**: Face Exodia himself on the Land map!

## Installation

1. Install [BepInEx](https://thunderstore.io/c/totally-accurate-battle-simulator/p/BepInEx/BepInExPack_TABS/)
2. Install [TGCore](https://thunderstore.io/c/totally-accurate-battle-simulator/p/Grad/TGCore/)
3. Download this mod
4. Extract to `TABS/BepInEx/plugins/`
5. Launch TABS and enjoy!

## Strategy Tips

### Budget Army (1500 gold):
- 5x Gun Man (750)
- 3x Plastek Man (600)
- 1x Best (400)

### Balanced Force (3000 gold):
- 2x Knight Arabe (1200)
- 4x Black Tiger (1200)
- 2x Best (800)

### Elite Army (5000+ gold):
- 1x King Arabe (1500)
- 3x Knight Arabe (1800)
- 5x Black Tiger (1500)

### Boss Mode:
- 1x Exodia + support units
- Unstoppable!
